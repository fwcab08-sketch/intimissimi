Knitwear

Do you want to refresh your drawer of tops and vests? Discover the vast range of round-neck and V-neck styles with wide or thin straps and all made from fabrics of differing textures. Our range of cottons includes standard stretch cotton, premium 100% lisle-yarn cotton. Being supremely soft and pleasant against the skin, microfibre is a another staple textile in our line-up. Opt for the fabric you are most attracted to and which feels good to wear so that you are at ease throughout the day. But our huge array is not only limited to fabrics, it also extends to colour palette. If you are looking for a classic t-shirt, you can choose from a gamut of restrained colours that no wardrobe should be without: white, black and various shades of grey. On the other hand, if you are looking for a piece of leisure-time menswear for chill-out days, there is a series of prints, patterns and distinctive details that gives elegance a new meaning. In our Tops section, you will be able to browse our selection of vests which look perfect under a shirt; to provide you with a broad choice, some styles have a more relaxed fit whilst others are figure-hugging. So, you want to be stylish at work too? Buy the IUMAN Intimissimi Uomo polo shirts. The perfect way to strike a balance between casual and dressy. The menswear collection comprises long-sleeve tops for layering under jackets, chunky winter sweaters or one of our sweatshirts for using at the gym or out jogging. With a hood or without, crew-necked or with a front-zip closure, in neutral hues or bright colors: choose the one you fancy and put it into your shopping bag. If you change your mind, you can always send it back to us free of charge and we will reimburse you without delay!
