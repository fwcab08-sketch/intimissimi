Men's Collection

At Intimissimi, we have designed our entire collection around the idea and lifestyle of the modern man. Fashionable and functional are the words that best describe our men's collection. Check out our men’s intimates and outerwear!

SOFT SUPPORTIVE UNDERWEAR

Your underwear is the first piece of clothing you put on to start your day, so start your morning right with a pair of Intimissimi’s underwear for men. To accommodate all preferences, our collection offers a variety of fits from boxers and briefs to boxer briefs. In fun patterns or solid colors, the choice is yours. Made from the finest cotton, our men’s underwear gives your body the best. Make the most of our multipack promotions for men!

SWIMWEAR FOR SUMMER

Is the weather getting warmer? Then it’s almost swimsuit season, and you must be in need of some new swimwear! Our collection includes swimming briefs and shorts, which are perfect for water sports and high activity thanks to their tight fit. Swim trunks provide a looser fit and are great for chilling at the beach or the pool during the summer. Check out our prints and patterns that you are sure to love!

THE BEST BASICS

Intimissimi’s men’s collection is your best bet for all the greatest basics that every man needs in his wardrobe. Intimissimi men’s cotton knitwear tops and undershirts are great on their own or layered under shirts and outerwear for added warmth. Our men’s socks meet all your needs: to match the formality of the office our dress socks are stylish and sophisticated, whereas patterned colorful socks are perfect for informal occasions.

PAJAMAS FOR MEN

Regardless of the weather or the season, Intimissimi has the men’s pajamas for you. Short pajamas are perfect during the summer season for some relief from the heat, whereas long pajamas will keep you cozy throughout the colder months. Wear our pajamas as a set, or have fun mixing and matching to create a look unique to you!
